Ashlyn O'Hara








Ashlyn O'Hara is an undergraduate journalism student who will attend the Missouri School of Journalism in Fall 2018. For the past year, she has been studying Journalism + Design and Politics at the Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City.

In addition to writing for her school paper, The New School Free Press, she also wrote for Our Hometown Magazine for two years, and was a contributing writer for the Los Angeles Times' HS Insider program.
