Ashlyn O'Hara








Ashlyn O'Hara is a state government reporter at The Columbia Missourian, where she has also worked as a public life reporter and attends the Missouri School of Journalism, where she studies data journalism.


From Fall 2017 to Sprin 2018, she studyied Journalism + Design and Politics at the Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City, where she wrote for her college newspaper, The New School Free Press.


She has also written for Our Hometown Magazine in San Diego, where she worked as an intern for two years, as well as for the LA Times' HS Insider program as a contributing author during Summer 2016.
