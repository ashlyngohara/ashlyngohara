Ashlyn O'Hara








Ashlyn O'Hara is an undergraduate student at The Eugene Lang College for Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City where she is a double major in Journalism + Design and Politics.

She wrote for Our Hometown Magazine for two years, was a contributing writer for the Los Angeles Times' HS Insider program, and currently writes for The New School Free Press, her school paper.
