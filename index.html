Ashlyn O'Hara








Ashlyn O'Hara is a public life reporter at The Columbia Missourian and attends the Missouri School of Journalism, where she studies investigative journalism.


For the past year, she has been studying Journalism + Design and Politics at the Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City, where she wrote for her college newspaper, The New School Free Press.


Ashlyn has also written for Our Hometown Magazine in San Diego, where she worked as an intern for two years, as well as for the HS Insider program as a contributing author during Summer 2016.
